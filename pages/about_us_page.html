History
Jockey's Conference Center has a rich history that spans several decades, making it a revered institution in the world of conferences and gatherings. Established in the bustling city center in 1974, it quickly became a prominent venue for intellectual discourse, professional events, and social gatherings. The founder, Jockey, said the center was built with the aim of providing a dedicated space for exchanging ideas, fostering innovation, and nurturing collaborations.
